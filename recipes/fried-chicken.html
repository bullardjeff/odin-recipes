Ingredients

  • One Chicken butchered into eighths
  • Flour
  • Eggs
  • Neutral oil like vegetable or peanut oil
  • Seasoning - Your preference and to taste

Steps

  1. Beat eggs and place into large dish
  2. Pour flour and seasoning onto two sheet pans
  3. Dip Pieces of chicken into 1st flour pan, then into egg mixture, then into 3rd flour pan
  4. Put oil into large dutch oven on stove and bring to 325 F
  5. Fry chicken in oil until golden brown and cooked to an internal temp of 165 F
