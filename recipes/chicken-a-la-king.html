Jeff's Phenomenal Chicken-a-la-King

This dish is fine!

While there are a few steps to this soggy, creamy chicken recipe, it's easy to make and has so much flavor. This dish cannnot be made ahead of time and does not freeze well, either before or after baking!

The best Chicken-a-la-King recipe is made with ingredients that you know, and it’s not difficult at all! All you’ll need for this easy Chicken-a-la-King recipe is one pan and a knife!

Ingredients

  • Chicken
  • A
  • LA
  • King
  • Queen (variation)

Steps

  1. First Sautee King (or Queen) on medium heat
  2. After your King is wilted, add chicken and turn heat up to medium-high
  3. When your chicken is almost cooked through, turn heat down to medium-low add your A and LA
  4. Once thickened, turn off the heat and let rest for 5 minutes
  5. Enjoy!
